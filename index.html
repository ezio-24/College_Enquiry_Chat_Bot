About Us

We are a happy College, because we believe happy children learn better. We are, as our valuable parents described us, "the College that children run to in the mornings". The warmth of our College is apparent the moment you walk through the doors. Our classrooms are seldom quiet,because they are filled with activity all through the day. Our teachers teach with enthusiasm and learning is a joyful experience of our children.

Our children learn together,because we have learnt through experience that a collaborative environment is the most fertile one for learning. We are a happy, international learning community, flourishing in an extraordinary facility which is unique to Amravati.

We have striven to build outstanding facilities and resources because we value our children and believe that they deserve only the best. This ideology permeates our campus and is reflected in our teachers and staff members, who, with unflinching faith and dedication partake and revel in nurturing the children. Together we strive to create happy citizens...ready to lead and make a difference!

From President's Desk

The moto of our College is

"Shine Bright thy High, be Humble"

'There is so much to learn to shine bright fly high and touch the sky and also to be humble in achieving all the goals in life'

"Opportunities come rarely but when they knock at the door one must make best use of them" I dream to see that every child walk I the soil of India with having complete faith in himself "small opportunities are often the beginning of great enterprises" And I hope to see this happening in and around my Taluka place.

It's said that 'Desire creates the power' and I've got that power. I've seen the vision 2 year back about having a beautiful College with beautiful vengeance pleasant atmosphere and the Teaching-Learning Process throughout in English.My heartly desire to educate each and every child no matter from which background he comes. I've decide to give complete and free education to the children who have lost their parents in between by any circumstances.

I always wanted that the farmers children to grow and I'm really fortunate to have their children as my students. It is indeed very difficult for the villagers and Taluka place people to admit their wards to Amravati Colleges as they have to pay heavy expenses in all sides. But as our College PR Pote College Amravati got opened in this Taluka place, it became affordable for the parents to admit their wards. We've excellent and educated Teaching and Non-Teaching Staff who strive hard to mould our future. I just want to conclude by saying...

'Lighten to Enlighten others'
GOD BLESS

Mr.Pravin Pote

President DRGIT & R

Our Values

OUR MISSION

To create a community of life-long learners working together in a caring, challenging and nurturing environment that encourages enthusiasm, international mindedness, independence and responsibility where academic and personal potential can be achieved.

VISION

We aspire to be a College from which students gain a sense of personal accomplishment, self-confidence, and a lifelong love for learning. By fostering critical thinking through the inquiry process, our students develop a broad range of competencies for their future endeavors. We value a strong partnership where parents contribute to the education and success of their children.

Our Focus

DRGIT & R run with the vision of integral development of each child enhancing them to be a versatile character with attention to academic per suite, humanism, tolerance , the adventure of ideas, quality learning , sports and Preserving the traditional Indian values .

Approach

  • A wholesome blend of traditional and modern pedagogy, with a deep Understanding of child Psychology.
  • Use of latest technology to make learning fun and easy.
  • Strong impetus on inculcating in children respect for Indian Culture & Value system.
